My dearest love,

Happy Birthday, My Love 💙

I don’t even know where to begin, because how do you put eight years of memories, laughter, tears, and love into just a few words?

You are not just my best friend, not just my soulmate — you are my home. From childhood innocence to everything we have grown through together, you have been my constant. We’ve faced so many storms, so many moments that could have broken us, but somehow, we always chose each other. And that’s what makes us… us.

From 2019 to 2026 — eight beautiful, messy, real years. Years filled with late-night talks, shared dreams, silent understanding, and holding on when things got hard.

You are my partner in crime, the one who knows my every side — the good, the bad, the chaotic — and still you chose to stay by my side. You have seen me at my weakest and loved me even harder. And I hope you know, I see you the same way.

You are enough, always. I just want you to feel how deeply you are loved. Not just today, but every single day. You deserve happiness, peace, success, and all the dreams you carry in your heart.

Thank you for growing with me. Thank you for staying. Thank you for being mine.

No matter where life takes us, one thing will never change — you will always be my person.

Happy Birthday, my forever.

Yours, always — Nigu 🫰🏻
